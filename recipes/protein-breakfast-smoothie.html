Protein Breakfast Smoothie

An easy breakfast smoothie that can be ready within minutes for consumption immediately or for on the go. The result is a smoothie that is high in protein and nutrients.

Kitchen Supplies Needed

    Blender

Ingredients Needed

  • 1 Peeled Banana

  • 2 Cups of Milk

  • 1/3 Cup of Oatmeal

  • 1 Scoop of Protein Powder

  • 1 Tablespoon of Peanut Butter

Preparation Steps

  1. Peel one ripened banana

  2. Measure out 2 cups of milk

  3. Pour the milk into the blender

  4. Drop the banana into the blender

  5. Measure 1/3 of a cup of oatmeal

  6. Measure one scoop of the protein powder of your choice

  7. Pour both the oatmeal and protein powder into the blender

  8. Drop one tablespoon of peanut butter in for texture
