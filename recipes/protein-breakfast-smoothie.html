Protein Breakfast Smoothie

An easy breakfast smoothie that can be ready within minutes for consumption immediately or for on the go. The result is a smoothie that is high in protein and nutrients.

Kitchen Supplies Needed

    Blender

Ingredients Needed

    1 Peeled Banana 2 Cups of Milk 1/3 Cup of Oatmeal 1 Scoop of Protein Powder 1 Tablespoon of Peanut Butter
